HOW TO START:

  Windows:  Double-click START.bat
  macOS:    Double-click start-palladium.command
  Linux:    Double-click start-palladium.sh
           (or run: bash start-palladium.sh)

That's it. No install needed — just Python or Java.

WHAT IT DOES:
  • Installs Docker + your chosen services (n8n, Postgres, Ollama, etc.)
  • Runs entirely from this USB — your data goes where you go
  • No cloud, no subscription, no lock-in
